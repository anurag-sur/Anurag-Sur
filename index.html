I’m interested in how ideas survive contact with reality.

Most don’t.

Anurag Sur

I build things.
Sometimes they work immediately.
Sometimes they fight back.

I like that part.

Design isn’t aesthetics.
It’s the moment where thinking is forced to become honest.

What I build

Products that have constraints, costs, tolerances, and consequences.

CAD. CAM. Prototyping. Manufacturing.
Tools to reduce uncertainty — not buzzwords.

I don’t chase perfection.
I chase things that work.

How I think

I’m suspicious of ideas that sound good but haven’t been tested.

Iteration over inspiration.
Constraints over freedom.
Progress over polish.

If something breaks, that’s information.

Things I’m proud of

— Building real products

— Learning manufacturing the hard way

— Staying curious after things go wrong

Most people stop when things get difficult.
I usually start paying attention.
